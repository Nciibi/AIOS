## Purpose

The Constitution Roadmap is the navigational guide to the AIOS Constitution. It explains how the Constitution is structured, how amendments are processed, how the Constitution relates to other tiers (Physics, Bible, RFCs), and how to read and interpret constitutional documents. This document does not define constitutional content — it defines how to navigate, understand, and participate in constitutional governance.

## Amendment Process

### Amendment Types

| Type | Scope | Approval Required | Example |
|------|-------|-------------------|---------|
| Law Amendment | Changes a Physics Law (0–9) | Sou majority + Security Council unanimous + Human override if Law 1 or 9 | Modifying Law 10 (Tenure) |
| Article Amendment | Changes a Constitution Article | Sou majority + Security Council unanimous | Adding a new Section to Article III |
| Section Amendment | Changes a Constitution Section | Security Council supermajority | Modifying Section 004 of Article II |
| Clerical Correction | Typo, formatting, clarification | Security Council simple majority | Fixing a cross-reference error |

### Amendment Lifecycle

```
Draft → Review → Vote → Ratify → Implement → Record
```

| Stage | Description | Duration | Owner |
|-------|-------------|----------|-------|
| Draft | Amendment text written | 1–30 days | Proposer |
| Review | Constitutional compliance check | 1–7 days | CLS |
| Vote | Approval vote by appropriate body | 1–14 days | Security Council / Sou |
| Ratify | Formal ratification with evidence | 1 day | CLS |
| Implement | Update Physics and Bible documents | 7–90 days | Affected entities |
| Record | Store in CKR with full history | 1 day | CKR |

### Amendment Proposal Requirements

Every amendment proposal must include:
1. **Amendment Text**: Exact wording of the proposed change
2. **Rationale**: Why this amendment is necessary
3. **Impact Analysis**: What changes downstream (Physics, Bible, code)
4. **Evidence Package**: Supporting evidence for the amendment
5. **Precedent Review**: How this amendment interacts with existing constitutional provisions
6. **Compatibility Assessment**: Verification that the amendment does not conflict with other Laws or Articles

### Voting Rules

| Vote Type | Threshold | Participants |
|-----------|-----------|--------------|
| Unanimous | 100% approval | All eligible voting entities |
| Supermajority | ≥ 66% approval | All eligible voting entities |
| Simple Majority | > 50% approval | All eligible voting entities |
| Human Confirmation | Explicit Human consent | Human authority |

## Constitution and Physics Relationship

The Constitution and Physics are complementary tiers:

| Aspect | Constitution | Physics |
|--------|--------------|---------|
| Type | Governance | Mathematics |
| Content | Rights, obligations, processes | Invariants, truths |
| Change Mechanism | Amendment via Article V | Amendment via constitutional amendment |
| Enforcement | Security Council, governance processes | Automated runtime enforcement |
| Audience | Entities, humans | System components |
| Examples | "Human is the source of authority" | "Every entity has exactly one identity" |

The Constitution defines *who* can do *what*. Physics defines *what must always be true*.
