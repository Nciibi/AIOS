## Purpose

The Autonomy Management System (AMS) is the central authority for autonomy level assignments across all AIOS entities. AMS owns the Level Registry â€” the single source of truth for every entity's autonomy level â€” and governs the two lifecycle transitions: progression (level upgrade) and regression (level downgrade). AMS ensures that every entity operates only at an autonomy level it has earned through demonstrated constitutional compliance, and that all level changes are audited, gated, and reversible.

## Core Operations

### Level Registry

The Level Registry is the persistent store of all entity-to-level assignments and serves as the authoritative source for ACE bounds resolution:

```
Registry Operations:
  Lookup:      getLevel(entity_id) â†’ AutonomyAssignment
  Assign:      setLevel(entity_id, level, reason) â†’ AutonomyAssignment
  Archive:     archiveEntity(entity_id) â†’ void
  QueryLevel:  queryByLevel(level) â†’ AutonomyAssignment[]
  Validate:    validateAssignment(entity_id, level) â†’ ValidationResult
```

Guarantees:
- Every entity registered in AIOS has exactly one entry
- Level values are always within [0, 4] and within entity type's max level
- Historical assignments are preserved in progression_history (append-only)
- Only AMS Progression and Regression engines may mutate entries
- Registry snapshots are emitted on every mutation for downstream consumers

## Cross-Cutting Concerns

### Security

Autonomy System operates under Law 8 (Verification-First) and Law 7 (Capability Bounds): every operation is authorized by the Security Kernel before execution, and the component never exceeds its declared capabilities. (Physics/008-Security.md)

### Evidence

Per Law 4 (Evidence), Autonomy System emits an evidence record for each significant state change - what changed, by whom, on what basis, with what outcome - delivered through ACF and persisted by EVS. (Physics/005-Events.md)

### Lifecycle

Per Law 6 (Lifecycle Compliance), Autonomy System instances follow the canonical LMS lifecycle (Draft -> Active -> Suspended -> Archived) and are terminated deterministically; orphan states are prevented. (Physics/006-Lifecycles.md)

### Capability Bounds

Per Law 7 (Capability Bounds), Autonomy System declares its capabilities at creation and operates only within them; capability expansion requires reauthorization through the Security Kernel. (Physics/007-Capabilities.md)
