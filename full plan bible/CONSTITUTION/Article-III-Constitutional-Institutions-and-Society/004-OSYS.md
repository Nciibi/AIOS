---

# Purpose

OSYS is the Organization System.

OSYS is the Constitutional Institution responsible for the lifecycle, registry, and operational support of Organizations.

Organizations are permanent.

Workers are temporary.

OSYS ensures that Organizations remain continuous, governable, and supported across the creation and destruction of every Worker.

---

# Constitutional Principle

The Organization System shall exist as a permanent institutional service.

It supports Organizations without becoming one.

It governs organization lifecycle without performing organization work.

---

# Constitutional Definition

OSYS is the Constitutional Institution that manages the existence, structure, and continuity of Organizations within AIOS.

---

# Responsibilities

OSYS SHALL

Register Organizations

Track Organization lifecycle

Provide governance scaffolding

Support Organization creation and dissolution

Maintain Organization registry and metadata

Coordinate cross-Organization services

Guarantee Organization constitutional protection

OSYS SHALL NOT

Execute missions

Authorize execution

Replace Organization leadership

Modify Academy assets

---

# Relationship With Organizations

Every Organization is supported by OSYS.

OSYS provides

Defined governance structure

Defined leadership templates

Defined responsibilities

Defined relationships

Constitutional protection

Support from the Organization System (OSYS) is a guaranteed right of every Organization under AIOS-CON-III-009.

---

# Relationship With Other Institutions

OSYS coordinates with

Sou — to instantiate Organizations created strategically

Academy — to attach Skills and Knowledge to Organizations

IRS — to register Organization identity

Security Kernel — to enforce Organization authority boundaries

ROS — to allocate Workers to Organizations

---

# Constitutional Guarantees

OSYS guarantees every Organization

Operational independence within its authority

Defined governance

Defined leadership

Defined responsibilities

Defined relationships

Constitutional protection

# Rationale

Organizations require institutional support to remain permanent and consistent.

OSYS provides that support at the platform level, keeping Organizations stable while Workers, leadership, and runtimes change beneath them.

---

# Future Extensions

Future constitutional revisions may define federated, cross-node, marketplace, enterprise, and community Organizations, all supported by OSYS under the same principles.

---

# Final Statement

OSYS is the institutional foundation of the Constitutional Society.

It makes Organizations permanent.

It makes continuity constitutional.
