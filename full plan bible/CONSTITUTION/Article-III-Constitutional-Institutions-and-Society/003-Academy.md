---

# Purpose

The Academy is the collective intelligence of AIOS.

It preserves the permanent intellectual assets of the platform: Skills, Knowledge, Experience, Mission Templates, Organization Templates, Policies, Benchmarks, and Training Material.

The Academy is the memory of AIOS.

Workers are temporary.

The Academy is permanent.

---

# Constitutional Principle

Knowledge belongs to AIOS, not to Workers.

Workers consume intelligence.

They do not own it.

---

# Constitutional Definition

The Academy is the Constitutional Institution responsible for preserving, verifying, and distributing collective intelligence.

The Academy is a subsystem, not merely storage.

---

# Permanent Assets

The Academy SHALL preserve

Skills

Knowledge

Experience

Mission Templates

Organization Templates

Policies

Benchmarks

Training Material

Prompt Libraries

These assets survive indefinitely.

They are not tied to any Worker, runtime, or model.

---

# Verification Requirement

The Academy SHALL accept only verified assets.

Knowledge requires verification of truth.

Experience requires

Problem

Solution

Evidence

Confidence Score

Verification

Reusable Pattern

Unverified observation shall never become permanent Knowledge or Experience.

---

# Distribution

The Academy distributes assets to Workers dynamically through Skills.

A Worker receives only the expertise required for its current Mission.

Distribution occurs through the Runtime Orchestration System (ROS) and the Security Kernel.

---

# Relationship With Organizations

The Academy preserves global intelligence.

Organizations preserve operational intelligence.

Workers receive temporary context.

Knowledge therefore exists at three levels

Academy

↓

Organization

↓

Worker

Global knowledge remains reusable.

Operational knowledge remains local.

Execution context remains temporary.

---

# Constitutional Guarantees

The Academy is guaranteed

Permanent preservation of verified assets

Independence from any runtime or model

Protection from unauthorized modification

Availability to every constitutional participant within its authority

# Rationale

Disposable intelligence is wasted intelligence.

By centralizing verified learning in the Academy, AIOS compounds capability across missions, workers, and generations.

The Academy is why AIOS becomes more capable over time rather than restarting from zero.

---

# Future Extensions

Future constitutional revisions may introduce additional Academy libraries, provided verification and permanence are preserved.

---

# Final Statement

The Academy is the memory of civilization inside AIOS.

Every Worker benefits from it.

Every Mission enriches it.

Knowledge shall never be lost.
